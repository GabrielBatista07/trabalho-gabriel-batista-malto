Maltodextrin


Sports Supplement:

Maltodextrin is a complex carbohydrate consisting of a chain of glucose. It is often used in sports supplements due to its ability to provide quick energy. When consumed, maltodextrin is digested and absorbed by the body efficiently, raising blood glucose levels and supplying immediate energy, making it a popular choice for athletes needing energy during intense workouts.

Food Industry:

In the food industry, maltodextrin is used as a thickening and texture agent. It can be found in a variety of products, from sauces to desserts, to enhance the consistency and texture of foods. Additionally, maltodextrin is a common ingredient in processed foods like cookies and cereals due to its ability to increase product volume and crispness.

Clinical Nutrition:

Maltodextrin also plays a significant role in clinical nutrition, being used in medical enteral nutrition products. These products are designed for patients who cannot eat normally and require a controlled source of calories and carbohydrates. Maltodextrin is a common choice in these products due to its digestibility and ability to provide consistent energy.
